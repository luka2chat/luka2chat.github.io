This Privacy Policy describes how Luka2Chat ("the Website", "We", "Us", or "Our") collects, uses, and protects Your information when You visit [https://luka2chat.com](https://luka2chat.com).

We are committed to protecting Your privacy. This Website is a personal developer portfolio and blog, and we collect minimal data to provide a good user experience.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

When You use the contact form on our Website, we collect:

- Your name
- Your email address
- The content of Your message

This information is collected solely to respond to Your inquiry and is not shared with third parties.

### Automatically Collected Information

When You visit our Website, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, time spent on pages, referring URLs, and navigation patterns
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and device type
- **Network Information**: IP address and general geographic location (country/region level)

This information is collected through standard web server logs and analytics tools to help us understand how the Website is used and to improve the user experience.

### Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to:

- **Essential Cookies**: Ensure the Website functions properly (e.g., remembering Your theme preference between light and dark mode)
- **Analytics Cookies**: Understand how visitors interact with the Website, such as which pages are most popular and how visitors navigate the site

You can configure Your browser to refuse cookies or alert You when cookies are being sent. Note that some features of the Website may not function properly without cookies.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- **To operate and maintain the Website**: Ensuring the site functions correctly and loads properly for all visitors
- **To respond to inquiries**: Replying to messages sent through the contact form
- **To improve the Website**: Analyzing usage patterns to enhance content, navigation, and performance
- **To ensure security**: Monitoring for and preventing unauthorized access or abuse

## Third-Party Services

Our Website may use the following third-party services:

- **Vercel**: Our hosting provider, which may collect standard web server logs. See [Vercel's Privacy Policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy).
- **GitHub**: We link to our open-source projects on GitHub. When You visit GitHub through our links, GitHub's privacy policy applies. See [GitHub's Privacy Policy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement).

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer Your personal information to outside parties.

## Data Retention

- **Contact form submissions**: We retain Your contact information and message only as long as necessary to respond to Your inquiry, after which it is deleted.
- **Analytics data**: Aggregated, anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely to track long-term trends. Individual-level data is not retained beyond the standard retention period of the analytics tools in use.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request information about what personal data we hold about You
- **Correction**: Request correction of any inaccurate personal data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of Your personal data
- **Opt-out**: Disable cookies through Your browser settings

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through the [Contact page](https://luka2chat.com/contact) on our Website.

Our Website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If You are a parent or guardian and believe Your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to remove that information.
